Who We Are

Building Legacies, One Property at a Time

Crest Homes & Properties Ltd was founded with a clear vision: to make premium real estate accessible, transparent, and rewarding for every Nigerian. Based in Benin City, Edo State, we serve clients locally and across the globe.

Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted name in the real estate industry, known for our integrity, professionalism, and commitment to delivering exceptional value to our clients.

Whether you're a first-time buyer, a seasoned investor, or part of the Nigerian diaspora looking to invest back home, Crest Homes is your reliable partner in property.
